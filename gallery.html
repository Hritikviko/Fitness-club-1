Indoor Swimming Pool

One of the 50 meters OLYMPIC swimming pool in Dublin, the outstanding design and mechanics allow to accommodate swimmers from all levels of ability by changing the depth of pool.
There are some benefits with the this tremendous and modern swimming pool :

  1. Fast, moderate and slow swimming lanes for all levels of swimming ability
  2. Divided into leisure swimming and massive family swimming areas
  3. Fun, purpose built toddler pool with fountains and sprinklers
  4. UV treated water with super low chlorine levels, that makes you enjoy the swim without worrying about red eyes and body discomfort
  5. Group and private swimming classes for beginners and intermediates
