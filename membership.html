OUR MEMBERSHIPS

At Motivation Sport & Fitness, we believe that Fitness should be an important part of your life. Our range of membership options will allow you to choose a membership that fits you the most to your need and you lifestyle

Daily Entrance Fee

Having a busy lifestyle but a holiday/free time once in a blue moon and wanna have exercises for the whole day to release stress? Daily membership is the most fit to you!

You are able to enjoy all the facilities for the whole day, no matter gym or swimming pool, with ONLY 8 € !

  • Adult - 8 €
  • Student - 5 €

Monthly Membership

Wish to have a regular exercises, but not sure how long you can maintain, or you are having a short-term target? We will suggest you to consider our monthly membership, which has several length of period for you to choose! You plan your exercise time, and we help to plan your exercise schedule!

  • 1 month - 65 € (Adult), 55 € (Student)
  • 2 months- 120 € (Adult), 100 € (Student)
  • 3 months- 170 € (Adult), 140 € (Student)
  • 6 months- 325 € (Adult), 285 € (Student)

Annually Membership

Planning for the long term target? Why don't you try our annually membership? Other than for all the benefits above, annually members will be given free fitness checkup periodically, to make sure your body improvement and plan a long and flexible exercise schedule due to your final target!

  • Adult - 625 €
  • Student - 480 €

Membership includes:

- Access to over 40 Fitness Classes a week (Excluded Daily Entrance)
- Access to all Club facilities
- Full support from our Fitness Team
- Planning personal exercise schedule

NOTE: For all the students who want to join our membership with cheaper price, you need a VALID STUDENT CARD as a proof during registration
