How to find Free Car Park in our club:

Convenient FREE parking is available for members at Motivation Sport and Fitness Club. It is exclusively for club members only. If you are new member and start for the first day, please don't hesitate to call us on 01-23456789. We will arrange empty parking for you.

  3. Finally, turn left at the corner again and go straight till the end
